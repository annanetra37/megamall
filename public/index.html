Where Culture Lives

Armenia's Heart,
Beating Inside.

Megamall Armenia was designed to be more than a mall. The rich history and culture of Armenia is its central theme — from national colors and geometric patterns woven into every surface, to the legendary "Night" fresco by Minas Avetisyan (1973), restored by Italian specialists from Fabrizio Lacoboni's team.

"The design symbolizes the beauty, luxury and abundance of the Armenian land — filling local visitors with pride and arousing interest in tourists."
